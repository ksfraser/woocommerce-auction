## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

#### Register for Free

3. Create password (minimum 8 characters, mixed case and number)

1. Check your email inbox for verification link
2. Click link to confirm email
3. Account ready to use

1. Go to **"My Account"** → **"Profile"**
2. Add:
   - [ ] First and last name
   - [ ] Shipping address
   - [ ] Phone number (optional)

## Buyer Guide

### Browsing Auctions

#### Find Auctions

**Method 1: Browse by Category**
1. Click **"Auctions"** in main menu
2. Select category (Collectibles, Electronics, etc.)
3. View available auctions

**Method 2: Search**
1. Enter search terms in search box (top)
2. Press Enter
3. Results show matching auctions

**Method 3: Filter Results**
1. Browse or search auctions
2. Use filters on left:
   - Price range
   - Auction type
   - Time remaining
   - Seller rating
3. Results update automatically

#### View Auction Details

**Click on auction to see**:
- Item description with images
- Current highest bid
- Bid history
- Time remaining
- Seller information and ratings
- Shipping details
- Return policy

### Placing Bids

#### Make an Offer

1. Click on auction item
2. Scroll to **"Place Bid"** section
3. Enter bid amount (must exceed minimum)
4. Note:
   - [ ] Current price: $XX
   - [ ] Minimum increment: $X
   - [ ] Your bid: $XX
5. Click **"Place Bid"**
6. Confirm bid in popup
7. Click **"Confirm Bid"**

#### Bid Confirmation

After placing bid, you'll see:
- ✓ "Bid accepted successfully"
- You are now the highest bidder
- Or "You were outbid by another bidder"

#### Automatic Bidding (Proxy Bidding)

By default, we use automatic bidding:

**How it works**:
- You enter your maximum bid
- System automatically bids for you
- You only pay when someone outbids you
- System increments minimum amount

**Example**:
- You bid: $100 (maximum)
- Current highest: $80
- Other bidder bids: $85
- System automatically bids: $90 for you
- You remain highest bidder
- Other bidder can bid higher if desired

### Winning an Auction

#### When You Win

You'll receive notification:
- Email confirming you won
- Item details
- Final price paid
- Seller contact information
- Payment instructions
- Shipping estimated time

#### Payment

1. Go to **"My Account"** → **"Winning Bids"**
2. Click item
3. Click **"Pay Now"**
4. Select payment method:
   - Credit card
   - PayPal
   - Bank transfer
5. Complete payment
6. Print invoice (optional)

**Expected Timeline**:
- Payment due: 7 days from end
- Seller ships: 3-5 days after payment
- Delivery: 3-14 days depending on location

## Auction Mechanics

### Auction Types

#### Standard (Ascending) Auction

**How it works**:
- Highest bid wins
- Price increases as bidders compete
- Automatic bidding increments minimum

**Best for**:
- Collectibles
- Antiques
- Unique items
- High-value goods

**Timeline**:
```
Day 1: 2 bids placed ($10, $15)
Day 3: Active bidding, now at $85
Day 6: 15 bids total, bidding slows
Day 7: Final hours, active bidding, ends at $120
```

#### Sealed Bid Auction

**How it works**:
- Bids hidden until auction ends
- Highest bid wins
- No bid increments shown

**Best for**:
- Liquidation sales
- Wholesale lots
- Sensitive pricing

**Timeline**:
```
Bids placed throughout (amounts hidden)
At end time: Results revealed
Winner: Highest bid
Losers: Don't see other bid amounts
```

#### Dutch Auction

**How it works**:
- Price drops over time
- First bidder at any price wins
- Helps move high-volume inventory

**Best for**:
- Bulk lots
- Clearance items
- Quantity sales

**Timeline**:
```
Start: $100 (Day 1)
Drop: $10 per day
Day 2: $90
Day 5: $60
Winner: First bidder at desired price
```

### Bid Increments

**System automatically increases bids by minimum**:

| Current Price | Minimum Increment |
|---------------|------------------|
| $0-$99 | $1.00 |
| $100-$499 | $5.00 |
| $500-$999 | $10.00 |
| $1,000-$4,999 | $25.00 |
| $5,000+ | $50.00 |

**Example**:
```
Current: $95
You bid: $100
You pay: $100 (minimum increment)

Later, outbid at $102
You bid automatically: $107 (+$5 increment)

Outbid at $115
You bid manually: $120
```

### Reserve Prices

**What is it?**
- Seller's minimum acceptable price
- Hidden from buyers
- Auction can end with "Reserve Not Met"

**How it works**:
```
Reserve: $50 (hidden)
Starting: $20
Highest bid: $45
Result: "Reserve Not Met" - Item doesn't sell

If highest bid: $55
Result: "Sold" - Meets reserve, sale complete
```

### Time Extension

**Last-Minute Bidding**:
- If bid placed in final 5 minutes: Auction extends 5 minutes
- Prevents "sniping" (last-second bids)

**Example**:
```
Auction ends: 2:00 PM
1:57 PM: Bid placed → Extends to 2:05 PM
2:04 PM: Bid placed → Extends to 2:09 PM
2:09 PM: No new bids → Final close at 2:09 PM
```

✓ "Secure2024Pass!"

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: How long should my auction run?**  
A: 7 days is optimal. Longer = more exposure, shorter = urgency. 3-5 days for fast-moving items.

**Q: What's a good starting price?**  
A: 50-75% of average sale price. Low starting price attracts more bidders.

**Q: Can I cancel an auction?**  
A: Yes, before first bid placed. After bid: contact seller or wait for completion.

**Q: How are bids calculated?**  
A: Starting + increments. Each bid increases by minimum increment until someone outbids.

### Bidding Questions

**Q: What if I'm outbid in final seconds?**  
A: Auction extends 5 minutes from last bid. You can place higher bid.

**Q: Can I retract my bid?**  
A: Only if bid placed within 1 hour and amount was error. Otherwise, no retractions.

**Q: What if I don't want to pay?**  
A: Payment expected within 7 days. Non-payment = negative feedback + possible account suspension.

**Q: Can I negotiate with the seller?**  
A: Only through messages. Auction price is set. Some sellers accept "Make an Offer" before starting.

### Shipping Questions

**Q: When will item ship?**  
A: After payment received. Typically 3-5 business days, stated in listing.

**Q: Can I change shipping address?**  
A: Ask seller before payment. Some allow, some can't. Must request within 24 hours of winning.

**Q: What if package is late?**  
A: Contact seller. If over 30 days: file claim for refund.

**Q: Are customs/duties included?**  
A: International shipping: Buyer responsible. Check before bidding.

### Payment Questions

**Q: Is my payment information safe?**  
A: Credit card & PayPal encrypted. We never store full card numbers.

**Q: What if payment fails?**  
A: Retry with different method or contact payment processor.

**Q: Can I pay partially?**  
A: No. Full payment due before seller ships.

### Seller Questions

**Q: What's the commission?**  
A: 10% of final sale price. Deducted from payout.

**Q: When do I get paid?**  
A: 7 business days after delivery. Via bank transfer or check.

**Q: What if buyer doesn't pay?**  
A: Re-list item. File unpaid case. Non-paying buyers get strikes.

**Q: Can I set a "Buy It Now" price?**  
A: No. Auction format only. Fixed price listings available separately.

## Best Practices

### For Buyers

1. **Read Descriptions Carefully**
   - Condition, flaws, included items
   - Shipping times and costs
   - Return policy

2. **Check Seller Rating**
   - Look for A/B ratings
   - Read recent feedback
   - Check for patterns in negative feedback

3. **Bid Smart**
   - Set maximum bid (don't bid emotionally)
   - Use proxy bidding
   - Bid early, not just last minute

4. **Communicate**
   - Ask questions before bidding
   - Request clarifications
   - Don't bid on unclear items

5. **Protect Your Account**
   - Use strong password
   - Enable 2FA
   - Verify suspicious activity

### For Sellers

1. **Accurate Descriptions**
   - Be honest about condition
   - Don't hide defects
   - Include all relevant details

2. **Quality Images**
   - Clear, well-lit photos
   - Show all sides
   - Highlight any damage
   - First image most important

3. **Reasonable Pricing**
   - Research comparable sales
   - Price competitively
   - Use reserves if concerned

4. **Fast Communication**
   - Answer questions within 24 hours
   - Be professional and helpful
   - Honest answers = more bids

5. **Proper Shipping**
   - Secure packaging
   - Tracking numbers
   - Insure valuable items
   - Ship quickly
